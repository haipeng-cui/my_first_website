  • What is Film Photography?
  • Film photography is photography that uses a progressively changing recording medium, which may be either chemical process based
  • What is film?
  • Photographic film is a strip or sheet of transparent plastic film base coated on one side with a gelatin emulsion containing microscopically small light-sensitive silver halide crystals. The sizes and other characteristics of the crystals determine the sensitivity, contrast and resolution of the film.
  • Why should we shoot with films?
  • Film photography is just straight up entertaining and exciting. It's fun to play around with settings, different cameras, and types of films and that's one good reason to go back to shooting with film
